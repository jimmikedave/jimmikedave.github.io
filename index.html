About Me





Like millions of other people around the globe, my relatively "normal" way of life was uprooted by COVID-19. I was originally an Operations Coordinator for an event production company, but the pandemic brought the entire event industry to a screeching halt. Since I was out of work, I decided to use this time to learn new skills and change my career path.


I chose to learn Full Stack JavaScript and became a developer, as I was fascinated by how websites and apps were made. As a Treehouse graduate, I learned the ins-and-outs of creating full fledge applications from the ground up, using JavaScript for the client side and the server side of my applications. This portfolio showcases my work as a Jr. Full Stack Developer. I aspire to work as a jr. developer creating user-friendly and interactive experiences on websites and apps. I thank you for taking the time to check out my portfolio!
